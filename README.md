Under the Surface is a 2D procedural mining exploration game that blends high-stakes resource management with an atmospheric, subterranean mystery. As an astronaut engineer deployed to the volatile crusts of newly discovered exoplanets, you must navigate a momentum-based physics system to drill through varying strata of rock, ice, and magma. The experience is designed around a "risk vs. reward" mechanic: the rarest materials are buried in the deepest, most hazardous layers, forcing you to balance your dwindling fuel reserves against the lure of a massive haul.
